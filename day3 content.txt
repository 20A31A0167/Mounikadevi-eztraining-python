append()-add an one element to the list-1.append(4)
extend()-add all elements of a list to another list-1.extend([5,6)]
insert()-insert an item at defined index(1,33)-1-index
remove()-removes an item from the list-1 remove(22)
pop()-remove & returns an element of given index-1.pop(3)
clear()-removes all items from the list
index()-returns the index of first matched item
count()-returns the countof the no of items passed 
sort()-sort items in list in ascending order
reverse()-reverse the order of items in list
copy()-returns a shallow copy of the list
